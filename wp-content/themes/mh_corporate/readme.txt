==================================
Description
==================================

MH Corporate WordPress Theme is a clean, modern and fully responsive WordPress theme with a light and minimalistic styling, which is perfectly suited for corporate and business websites. This flexible business WordPress theme is ideal to promote your products and services or to run a corporate blog.
